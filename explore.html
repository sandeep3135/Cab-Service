Chitrakote Waterfall

Known as the "Niagara Falls of India," Chitrakote is the widest waterfall in the country, especially majestic during the monsoon season.

Tirathgarh Waterfall

Located in the Kanger Valley National Park, this multi-tiered waterfall is surrounded by lush greenery and is a favorite among nature enthusiasts.

Bhoramdev Temple

Bhoramdeo Temple is an ancient Shiva temple near Kawardha, known as the "Khajuraho of Chhattisgarh" for its beautiful architecture and carvings.
